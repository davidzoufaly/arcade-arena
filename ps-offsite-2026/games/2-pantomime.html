Pantomime

The camera watches how you move. Your team must mimic the target pose with speed and precision to score.

Goal: Match each target pose with your body. The reference pose is shown beside the video so you can see what to aim for. Each pose has multiple checks — get every check above 85 %, then hold the pose still for the required time (1.2 s easy, 1.5 s medium, 2 s hard). Wobbling resets the hold timer.

Speed wins: each pose is worth up to 100 points — half from how clean your form is, half from how fast you lock it in. Nail it instantly = near 100; dawdle until the timer almost runs out = far fewer. Don't overthink — strike the pose and lock it fast.

8 poses per run, escalating from easy to hard. 25 s per pose. Run out of time = pose is skipped (zero points). Players take turns — one person poses each round, the rest direct. 2 attempts — best score counts 🏆.
