Mission briefing

An Alien craft has locked onto your data center from low orbit. It isn't observing — it's harvesting.

The invaders call themselves GoodStaff Must Not Survive. They've partnered with Skynet to automate Earth's takeover: destabilize infrastructure, extract secrets, and convert humans into a controllable energy grid — power plants in a planetary machine.

Your facility is one of the last nodes still resisting. The Alien jammer is killing power, corrupting signals, and sealing physical access. Logs are distorted. Documents are hidden or replaced with decoys. An "Oracle" AI guards the final access key.

You are the Incident Response Unit: Project Future is US! Throughout the day, teams must complete missions to collect Alien Tech Fragments (ATFs). At the end, you'll use them to assemble the Planet Shield Activation Key and stop the conversion protocol before it reaches critical mass.
