Dino Run

Control the runner together with your hands — the more players coordinate the 'jump' gesture, the higher it leaps. Teamwork becomes lift.

Goal: Steer the runner 🦖 with your hands in front of the laptop camera — two players play at once. The runner jumps ⬆️ and ducks ⬇️ past obstacles 🌵. First, a 20-second calibration: the whole team raises one hand each so the game can size the team — a team of 2 plays the entire run together, a bigger team plays in ~20-second waves 💨 and rotates two players in each wave (10-second breather to swap). Each run then opens with a ~10-second warm-up to practise the gestures — no obstacles appear until it ends. Open palm ✋ = jump — it fires the instant one player raises a palm. Both players raise palms together while in the air = a double jump for extra height. Make a fist ✊ to duck. Hold a victory sign ✌️ to stay ready (no jump, no duck). Your score is the number of obstacles passed. 5 attempts, best score counts 🏆.
